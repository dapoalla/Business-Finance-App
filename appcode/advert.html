Designed with Christian business owners in mind. The app automatically calculates 10% of your profit on completed invoices, ensuring you never miss an opportunity to give back.

Access your financial dashboard from anywhere. The clean, dark-mode UI is optimized for your phone, making it easy to manage your business on the go.

Lightweight & Secure

No bloat. This is a fast, efficient web app that runs on standard cPanel hosting. Your data is kept secure and private within your own hosting environment.
